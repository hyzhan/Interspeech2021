Audio samples from "Improve Cross-Lingual Text-To-Speech Synthesis on Monolingual Corpora with Pitch Contour Information"

Abstract: Cross-lingual text-to-speech (TTS) synthesis on monolingual corpora is still a challenging task, especially when many kinds of languages are involved. In this paper, we improve the cross-lingual TTS model on monolingual corpora with pitch contour information. In this model, we propose a method to obtain pitch contour sequences for different languages without manual annotation, and extend the Tacotron-based TTS model with the proposed Pitch Contour Extraction (PCE) module. Our experimental results show that the proposed approach can effectively improve the naturalness and consistency of different kinds of mixed-lingual synthesis utterances.
